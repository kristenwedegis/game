Who do you choose to follow?

Journey alongside the mighty Hercules as he sets off to complete his first labor, slaying the Nemean Lion.
Follow along as Daedalus, the famous Greek artist, and his son Icarus try to escape the island of Crete in one piece.
Experience a hidden wonder of the Underworld with the kind-hearted Goddess Persephone, or dare to meet the heartless Hades in the very domain he rules over.
